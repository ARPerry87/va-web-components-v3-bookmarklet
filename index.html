📌 VA Web Components Bookmarklet

Drag the a-ref below to your bookmarks bar:

🔍 Show/Hide VA Web Components

How to Use

  1. Open this Gist in your browser via raw GitHub URL (like gist.github.com/…/raw/va-web-components-bookmarklet.html).
  2. Drag the blue button to your bookmarks bar.
  3. Go to any page on your local vets-website (e.g. http://localhost:3001).
  4. Click the bookmark – borders & labels will appear around all va‑* components.
  5. Click again to toggle it off — or refreshing the page removes it entirely.

Tip for team members: Bookmarklets work in most browsers—no permissions needed.
